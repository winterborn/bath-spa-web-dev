Why study with us?

Nature!

Do you like nature? Great! As you walk around our grounds, you will quickly learn that nature here does not like you!

Get up close and personal with our Weeping Willow Tree and discover that you never truly get a day off from life-threatening situations.

Are you a fan of spiders? We have giant hairy beasts that you can chat to, before experiencing the thrill of running for your life!

Danger!

Sure, you could go to a regular school and feel safe... Or you could come to Hogwarts where we will actively throw you into life-threatening situations.

We aim to ease you in gently, where you will explore the delights of learning spells and to ride a broom. From here, we will have you fighting an assortment of dangerous creatures. You may even get to risk your life against huge dragons for sport!

The Chosen One Package!

Upgrade to our Chosen One package and we will provide you with your very own arch enemy, who will stop at nothing to eviscerate you!

You'll also have an amazing time learning about the politics of the wizarding world, as your enemy takes control over government and turns everyone against you.
